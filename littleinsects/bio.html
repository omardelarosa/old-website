bio

  

 

 

 

Little Insects has been Omar Delarosa's principal music recording project since Summer 2008. The music has been described as"anti-charisma taken to absurd lengths" by the Village Voice, but Delarosa just likes to call it "bedroom pop with folk and electronic influences." Live performances alternate between small band performances featuring Colin Menzel and Daniel Kang and solo performances of Delarosa singing and playing guitar or piano.
